https://docs.python.org/3/library/codecs.html#standard-encodings

因为老版windows的console不支持utf-8,所以要在console里显示汉字
要把cp设为gbk
并且在编译时指定 "-fexec-charset=GBK" 的编译参数

gbk=cp936

console cp=cp65001 时:
"一些utf-8汉字".encode('utf8').decode('gbk','ignore') == '涓浜utf-8姹瀛'
"涓浜utf-8姹瀛"

console cp=cp437 时:
"一些utf-8汉字".encode('utf8').decode('437','ignore') == 'ΣY?ΣU?utf-8???σ?ù'
"ΣY?ΣU?utf-8???σ?ù"
